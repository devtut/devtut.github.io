Syntax

Section titled “Syntax”
  • reduce(function, iterable[, initializer])

Parameters

Section titled “Parameters”

|Parameter|Details |---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|--- |function|function that is used for reducing the iterable (must take two arguments). (positional-only) |iterable|iterable that’s going to be reduced. (positional-only) |initializer|start-value of the reduction. (optional, positional-only)
